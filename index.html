Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for KingSukh Guest House

  • 01What are the check-in and check-out times?

    Check-in is at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

  • 02Is parking available at the guest house?

    Yes, we offer free on-site parking for our guests. Please inform us in advance if you require a parking space.

    Our rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, complimentary toiletries, and a private bathroom.
